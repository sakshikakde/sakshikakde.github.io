About Me

I'm an AI/ML Engineer at Quidient, a company specializing in 3D reconstructions. I have a master's degree in Robotics from the University of Maryland, College Park, United States, and a bachelor's degree from Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology, India.

I have a keen interest in Computer Vision and Deep Learning. Currently, I'm actively exploring the intersection of physics-informed rendering and deep Learning. My main objective is to leverage these techniques to understand material properties, manipulate light fields, and generate novel views and relighting effects. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to work with Neural Radiance Fields, a cutting-edge technique that allows us to represent complex 3D scenes with neural networks.

On this website, I aim to share my knowledge, insights, and projects related to Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Machine Learning. Feel free to reach out if you have any questions, ideas, or opportunities for collaboration.
